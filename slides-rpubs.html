Next Word Predictor

Coursera Data Science Capstone Project, Bertrand Rigaldies, October 3rd, 2016

  • An N-grams-based probabilistic model to predict the next most likely words in an english sentence (See example of R output below)
  • Using Maximum Likelihood Estimates (MLE), and Stupid Backoff Algorithm-based weighted probabilities;
  • Achieving about 25% prediction success rate;
  • Test drive it here.
